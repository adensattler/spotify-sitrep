Privacy


Aden Sattler built SITREP as an open source app using the Spotify Web API. This app is provided to all users at no cost. This page is intended to outline and disclose how your information is used by my service.

In choosing to use this service you agree to the use of your Spotify account information including your username, top tracks, artists, and other statistics. You information will not be collected, saved, or shared with anyone nor will I have access to your data. All information gathered is used for the sole purpose of compiling your situation report.

That said, if you would like to remove SITREP's permissions from accessing your account data you can do so here. Locate SITREP on your apps page and hit the "Remove Access" button.
